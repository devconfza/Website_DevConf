Clifford de Wit

Keynote 08h45

The age of the modern Developer

This talk will cover the evolution of the developer and how as developers today we are in such a privileged position to have impact in our businesses but also on the world and society.
In his current role Clifford is the CTO and co-founder of a new Digital business within the Metrofile group, Dexterity Digital. Dexterity is focused on building out leading Digital Transformation products to help companies move to the digital world and gain value out of new technologies such as Cloud Computing and Machine Learning.
