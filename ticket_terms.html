Ticket Purchasing

Tickets are sold on behalf of DevUG by Quicket or Fizz Marketing. Do not purchase tickets from any source other than these two authorised agencies. Tickets purchased from any other source may be unauthorised copies and may be refused admittance to the conference. All fees become payable at the time the booking for the conference is made. If this fee remains unpaid at the time of the conference, you may be denied entry to the conference or workshop, with no reduction in your liability to pay the fee. For manual ticket bookings through Fizz Marketing, tickets will only be reserved for 3 weeks from the date of invoice sent. Should payment not be received by the deadline the tickets will be released. The cut off for manual ticket bookings through Fizz Marketing is two weeks before the event date. Look after your ticket(s) as you would cash. It must be presented to gain admittance to the conference. The code on the ticket uniquely identifies this ticket and the purchaser and can be redeemed only once. Multiple copies of this ticket will not allow multiple entries. If multiple copies of this code are presented at the conference, Dev UG and its authorised agencies may refuse entry to all ticket holders presenting tickets with this code.

Manual Purchases

There is a 5% admin fee applied to all manual ticket purchases, except for additional sponsor ticket purchases. Quicket offers the ability to purchase tickets and pay with credit card, EFT, SnapScan, Zapper or BitCoin. If you need an invoice to arrange EFT payment, you can do this directly on Quicket and avoid any admin fee. If you need more time to pay, please email us with your invoice number and we can arrange additional time.

Transfers, Cancellations and Refunds

In the event of the conference being cancelled, the purchase will be refunded via the same payment method with which the original purchase was made within 3 weeks of the request. In the event of a postponement, purchased tickets for the original date will be valid for the new date unless otherwise notified. Purchasers have the option to seek a refund for the value of the purchased ticket(s) for a postponed event by contacting us after the announcement of the new conference date. In the event of the refund being authorised for a postponed event, the refund will be processed via the same payment method with which the original purchase was made within 3 weeks. If you have booked for the conference and are unable to attend, you may transfer your ticket to another person by logging into Quicket and updating the ticket details not less than 72 before the conference. To cancel a booking for a conference, please email us.

  • A full refund can only be provided if notice is received at least 45 days before the event.
  • A 50% refund is offered if notice is received at least 15 days before the event.
  • A 25% refund is offered if notice is received at least 5 days before the event, after which no refund will be issued.
These timelines apply for the original event date, and if the event is postponed.
All refunds are less applicable bank changes and Quicket booking fees.
