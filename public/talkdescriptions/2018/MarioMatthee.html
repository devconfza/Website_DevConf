About the talk

Once upon a time Martians and Venusians met, started working in agile software development teams together, and had happy relationships because they respected and accepted their differences. Then they came to Earth and amnesia set in. (They forgot that they were from different planets.) Based on years of successful counselling of software developers and testers, Software Developers Are from Mars; Testers are from Venus has helped one team (somewhere) transform their relationships. Now viewed as a tongue-in-cheek talk on modern software development, this phenomenal discussion has helped software developers and testers realise how different they are and how to communicate their needs in such a way that conflict doesn't occur and relationships are given a chance to grow.
