About the talk

In this talk, I’ll take a deeper dive into graphing, what it can do, where to use it and where not to. Allowing you to add it to the tools you already have in your toolbox. We’ll take a look at graphs and the tooling in today’s world that allows us to make use of graphs in different ways. I’ll be doing a lot of demo’s along the way. Talk outline:

  • What is a graph and where did it come from. Graphs are not a new concept but why should I care about them.
  • We’ll take a look at where graphs are used and what types of problems they solve.
  • GraphQL: Facebook’s mobile apps have been powered by GraphQL since 2012. A GraphQL spec was open sourced in 2015. We’ll take a look at: how we can describe your data, ask for what you want, get everything you need in a single request, GraphiQL - A powerful developer tool, Evolving you API without versions, Limitations.
  • DGraph: A database built for graphs that uses GraphQL for a query language. We’ll take a look at DGraph as a solution and how it moves past some of the limitations mentioned before.
