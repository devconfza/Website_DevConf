About the talk

What is the cost of our culture's addiction to software? How much fuel is needed to produce enough power to keep that docker container running? I'm no bunny-hugger, but I'll share some of my research and conclusions on a cause many of us don't think about - the environmental impact from our insatiable need for software, and what we as developers can do to help. Don't miss it.

About the speaker

'm a dedicated software developer at DVT and tend to focus on Java \ DevOps during working hours. On weekends I enjoy catching some fresh air by hiking or pottering in the garden. When I'm not getting my hands dirty, I spend my time reflecting on the nature of software and our complex world.
