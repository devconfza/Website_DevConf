About the talk

By adopting modern application development practices, companies transform the way applications are built and delivered to delight customers and outpace competitors. In this talk, we describe how you can deliver and maintain high-quality, innovative applications faster than ever, with solutions that increase developer productivity and operational efficiency. We will look at how to develop and deliver cloud-native applications faster with application services, microservices, containers, APIs, and more.

About the speaker

Phillip is an accomplished Software developer and architect offering experience in creating systems in various industries, ranging from banking, insurance, rewards programs, retail and mobile. He has extensive experience in full stack development and prefers to be active in all parts of the SDLC. From conceptualizing to running in production. Phillip has a passion for clean code and evolutionary architecture and loves Java, Linux and Open source.
