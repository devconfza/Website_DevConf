About the talk

Developing a product is fun, these products connect us, and accepting payment across countries is one of many ways to scale a product.
This session will concentrate on showing developers how to integrate multiple African payments flows (such as MPESA, MOBILE MONEY, CARD, USSD and more) into products via One API.

About the speaker

I am an African Tech Educator. I am particularly hyped on Fintech and education in Africa. I am currently working with flutterwave on developing and enhancing payment systems, as well as contribute to developing and maintaining developer communities in Africa.
