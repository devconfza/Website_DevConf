About the talk

If those who can’t remember history are doomed to repeat it, maybe it’s time for us to look back at inspiring women who shaped the modern world. Featuring heroines such as Ada Lovelace, Margaret Hamilton and Grace Hopper, let’s dig into the history of women in technology, in order to look forward.

About the speaker

Christina Burger is a senior software engineer from Durban. She specialises in front-end development for online games, focusing on performance optimisation and investigating new technologies. She is passionate about software craftsmanship, life-long learning and supporting tech communities. She holds a master's degree in Computer Science and a love-hate relationship with JavaScript. When she is AFK, she's usually reading, sewing or irritating her cat.
