About the talk

Discuss how Netstar moved from 300 msgs per second to 3000 msgs per second in 2 years and the challenges we faced in implementing Kubernetes, Cassandra and Kafka on-prem, and how we've simplified the deployment and maintenance of the architecture and stack at scale in Azure.

About the speaker

Proud father and husband. 20 years of development experience of which 15 years in the high volume and velocity transaction environment of Netstar. Started as a C# and SQL MS stack but recently moved to open source and have become an open source and containerised evangelist.
