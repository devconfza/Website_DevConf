About the talk

The power of tech is not tech. It’s people. The people who dream it, code it, eat, breathe, sleep, live it every minute of every day as they pour their hearts and souls into making, shaping, crafting… creating. It’s at this intersection of creativity and technology that the magic happens. So how do you create an environment where these creative technologists can thrive? Overthrowing the yoke of legacy, Quraish Behari takes a look at fear, failure and freedom in this thought-provoking session on culture and creativity.

About the speaker

Quraish Behari is the CEO of Derivco, a leading software development and IT services company in the online gaming world, employing over 1500 of the brightest minds in the industry across six offices in South Africa, Costa Rica, Australia and Isle of Man. Having started his career as a Software Developer, Quraish quite literally learnt the “business” of software development from the inside out. Coming from a technology background, he brings a unique point of view to the boardroom table as he works to shape a culture where technologists can thrive.
