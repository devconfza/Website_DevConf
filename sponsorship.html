Introduction

Dear Potential Sponsor,

DevConf, the conference for professional South African software engineers, is now in its fifth year of planning, aiming to bring together the entire local software engineer community for a full day of in-depth discussions in two cities. DevConf 2020 is aiming to reach over 1250 developers.

By providing the right sponsorship platforms, we aim to work with our sponsors in providing delegates with further event benefits such as networking, career discussions and opportunities.

What’s new in the DevConf 2020 sponsorship packages? We are excited to launch new stands which we feel will improve the look and feel of the networking space and also help differentiate between the different sponsor levels. Furthermore with the continued effort to provide a eco-friendly event, we have introduced branded water bottles and will be placing water coolers on the top tier sponsor to attract delegates to these stands. In addition we will adding laptop stickers for the top tier partners. We have also increased the discount to purchase additional tickets from 10% to 12.5% so that our valued sponsors can send more of their team to be part of the experience!

For 2020 we are also going to be rewarding the best rated sponsor by offering them an 8% discount on all their 2021 sponsorship packages.

What isn't changing for DevConf 2020 is the price! We are very excited to announce that all sponsorship packages are staying at their 2019 price or have been lowered.

It goes without saying that DevConf 2020 simply wouldn’t be possible without the support of our valued sponsors. As in previous years, we hope to see many of our returning sponsors and hope to welcome new sponsors onboard to provide our delegates with an even greater experience in 2020.

We look forward to working with you!

Candice Herodotou & Robert MacLean

DevConf Organisers & Directors of The Developer User Group

Attendee Profile

The event targets professional software engineers. These people include anything from solo developers to anyone who work in mid to large sized organisations who develop systems of all sizes for enterprise companies.

The event aims to attract attendees across industries such as financial services, media houses, telco’s, retail etc…

Attendees range from junior to senior developers and managers from SDM up to CIO’s as well as smaller amount of DevOps/Operability engineers.

Key 2019 Headline Statistics

  • 49% of attendees identified as senior level, 35% at intermediate and 12% at junior level.
  • 75% of attendess are developers, followed by 11% managers.
