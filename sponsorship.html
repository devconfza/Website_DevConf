Attendee Profile

DevConf targets professional software developers, from all kinds of organisations. From independent developers to anyone who works in mid-to-large-sized organisations who develop solutions of all sizes for enterprise companies.

DevConf has a track record of being the event for senior software developers with 65% of attendees being of a senior or higher level in 2023 and an average working experience of 12 years.

The event does attract a broad range of people beyond developers with Tech Leads (15% in 2023), DevOps (2% in 2023), executives (7% in 2023), managers (11% in 2023) and QAs (4% in 2023) being in attendance.
