Attendee Profile

DevConf targets professional software developers, from all kinds of organisations. From independent developers to anyone who works in mid-to-large-sized organisations who develop solutions of all sizes for enterprise companies.

DevConf has a track record of being the event for senior software developers with 63% of attendees being of a senior or higher level in 2022 and an average working experience of 11 years.

The event does attract a broad range of people beyond developers with DevOps (6% in 2022), executives (5% in 2022), managers (11% in 2022) and QAs (3% in 2022) being in attendance.
